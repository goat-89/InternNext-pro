# Legal Review Checklist

The legal pages in this repository are structured product drafts, not legal
advice. A qualified professional must review and approve them for the operating
entity, jurisdictions, processing activities, and commercial model before
public launch.

## Operator And Contact Details

- [ ] Confirm the full legal entity name and trading name.
- [ ] Add the registered office and legally required business identifiers.
- [ ] Name the grievance officer and publish the approved contact channel.
- [ ] Confirm the support, privacy, billing, and legal notice channels.
- [ ] Define response and escalation timelines for complaints and appeals.

## Document Control

- [ ] Approve the effective date and version for every legal document.
- [ ] Define how users receive notice of material changes.
- [ ] Preserve prior versions and the approval record.
- [ ] Confirm governing law, venue, and dispute-resolution wording.
- [ ] Review accessibility and language requirements for intended users.

## Privacy And Data

- [ ] Map collected data to a specific purpose and lawful processing basis.
- [ ] Approve retention and deletion periods for profiles, resumes,
  applications, support, payments, security logs, and audit records.
- [ ] Confirm account-deletion exceptions and user-facing timelines.
- [ ] Review minors, age assurance, and parent or guardian consent controls.
- [ ] List processors, subprocessors, hosting regions, and international
  transfers.
- [ ] Review cookie categories, consent controls, and analytics behavior.
- [ ] Confirm data access, correction, deletion, grievance, and nomination
  procedures.

## Marketplace And Billing

- [ ] Review employer verification, listing moderation, candidate privacy, and
  prohibited conduct terms.
- [ ] Approve plan descriptions, recurring or one-time billing language,
  taxes, cancellation, refunds, and failed-payment handling.
- [ ] Confirm consumer disclosures and complaint handling.
- [ ] Review community, acceptable-use, safety, suspension, and appeal rules.

## Final Approval

- [ ] Verify all public links and contact details in the deployed environment.
- [ ] Record counsel name, approval date, scope, and outstanding limitations.
- [ ] Obtain operator and product-owner sign-off.
- [ ] Schedule a review after material product, provider, or legal changes.
